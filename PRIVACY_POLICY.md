# Privacy Policy for ClipVault

**Effective Date:** February 23, 2026

## Overview

ClipVault is a privacy-first, offline-by-default clipboard manager designed to run locally on your device. We believe your clipboard data belongs exclusively to you.

## Information Collection & Usage

**ClipVault does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information, clipboard data, or telemetry.**

- **Local Storage:** All clipboard history (text, links, code snippets) is stored strictly locally on your individual device. 
- **No External Servers:** The application has no server backend. It does not communicate with, nor send your data to, any external servers or third-party services.
- **No Analytics:** We do not track crash reports, usage metrics, or user behavior.

## Permissions

To function as a clipboard manager, ClipVault requires system permissions to monitor your local system clipboard. This permission is used exclusively in real-time to save your copied snippets to your local history. This data never leaves your device.

## Contact

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy or the application's security architecture, please open an issue on our [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/Charlesprakasam/clipvault).
